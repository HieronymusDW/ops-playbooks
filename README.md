# Operational Playbooks

A collection of practical frameworks and playbooks used to build and scale operations in growing organizations.

---

## Weekly Operating Rhythm

- KPI review and performance tracking  
- Identification of risks and blockers  
- Clear prioritization of actions  
- Ownership and accountability per topic  

---

## Scaling Teams

- Define clear roles and responsibilities  
- Build leadership layers early  
- Create predictable reporting and decision cadence  
- Reduce dependencies and increase autonomy  

---

## Performance Management

- Establish measurable KPIs  
- Review on fixed cadence (weekly / monthly)  
- Link performance to clear actions  
- Maintain transparency across teams  

---

## Focus

These playbooks are based on real-world experience scaling a €30M / 90 FTE organization and are continuously refined through practical use.
